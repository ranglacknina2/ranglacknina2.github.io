Design Competition:
Sanitary Facility

Kemmlit is a market-leading company for sanitary and individual furnishing. They offer a variety of high quality solutions for custom-made changing rooms, toilet stalls, lockers etc.

Previous Project Next Project

You have to activate the sensor above the toilet to rinse. This way to avoid to touch the area where bacterias accumulate. For the surface we chose to use frosted glass, its hygienic, easy to clean and resistant to solvents. Also the appearance let the stalls seem light and fresh.

Doors are always open, they only close when the door mechanism is activated by the sensor located in the cabin. By closing the cabin door, 2 coat hooks fall out of the niche. You can hang your jacket or bag on it.

Under a two-week deadline
we won 1st place in this competition
