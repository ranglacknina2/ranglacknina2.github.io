Bachelor Thesis:
Restaurant & Café

The goal of this project was to redesign and enlarge the restaurant and the associated food market. Inexpensive suggestions for general improvements regarding the design were desired. The courtyard café invites you to meet up with family or friends in the lounge.

Previous Project Next Project

By selecting modern furniture and implementing an antiquated wood texture
the design offers a rustic style reflecting the agricultural sector.

In the sunroom, the seating area was enlarged to add a café. Individually separated areas
also give guests the opportunity to make reservations for a celebration or a larger group to enjoy their privacy.
