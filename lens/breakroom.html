Bosch Rexroth:
Breakroom

The task for this project was to redesign their large breakroom capable of seating 40 people. The room was also intended to work as a functional conference room.

Previous Project Next Project

First, we designed the break room to have a kitchen
as a means for employees to cook their meals or heat them up.

With the simple structure of the furniture and color selection,
our design gave the break room a modern and welcoming atmosphere.

Since the room did not offer much space for sitting,
we designed the room to have fold-out tables and chairs that can be pulled out if necessary.
