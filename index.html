About Me

Hey there and welcome to my website! My name is Nina and I am an interior designer. On this website I share my projects and attempt to exhibit the passion, style, and precision, I bring to my craft. At the end of 2019, my green card was approved, and I moved to the US from my home country in Germany to explore this beautiful land and the exciting amount of opportunities that exist here.

I love what I do. My passion for design can be illustrated as the joy I experience when seeing an idea become a reality. It also makes me proud to create something with my own hands to make others happy and able to call it their “home.” As an Interior designer, I am a specialist in transforming proportions and spatial structures. From conception to planning until realization, I devote myself to each step of the process to ensure my clients’ expectations are always met. In my free time, I enjoy outdoor activities, woodwork and various types of art such as photography and music.
I hope you enjoy!
- Nina
